CRICKET

Cricket is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each.
Cricket has been an established team sport for several centuries.
It originated in its modern form in England and is popular mainly in the present
and former members of the Commonwealth.In some countries in South Asia
including India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka,cricket is a popular sport.
Cricket is also a major sport in England and Wales, Australia, New Zealand,
South Africa, Zimbabwe and the English-speaking countries of the Caribbean
which are collectively known in cricketing parlance as the West Indies


The ICC (The International Cricket Council) is the global governing body for cricket.
Representing 108 members, the ICC governs and administrates the game
and works with our members to grow the sport.
The ICC is also responsible for the staging of all ICC Events.
The ICC presides over the ICC Code of Conduct, playing conditions,
the Decision Review System and other ICC regulations.
It also appoints all match officials that officiate
at all sanctioned international matches.
